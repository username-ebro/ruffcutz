Ruff Cutz Ethical Musical Collective Announces Release of New Single From Hit Album

Latest offering now available wherever the new single is available

NEW ORLEANS, LA — June 1, 2026 — The Ruff Cutz Ethical Musical Collective ("Ruff Cutz," "the Collective") today announced the release of a new single, the latest offering from the group's hit album, released August 30.

"We are proud to announce that the single has been released," said a spokesperson for the Collective. "It is a song. It is available."

The single represents a significant milestone in the Collective's ongoing rollout strategy, under which individual songs from the hit album are made available to the public on a rolling basis. Industry observers note that the single is a track that appears on the album.

The Collective confirmed that the single may be listened to. Listeners are advised that the experience of the single is best achieved by listening to it.

The new single is available wherever the new single is available. The hit album, released August 30, remains a hit.

About Ruff Cutz Ethical Musical Collective

Ruff Cutz is a New Orleans-based musical outfit playing tunes that make you feel. The Collective remains committed to its mission of music for the movement. Recordings are available on Bandcamp at turbozone.bandcamp.com.
