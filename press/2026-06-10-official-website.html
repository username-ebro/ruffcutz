Ruff Cutz Ethical Musical Collective Confirms Official Web Presence at ruffcutz.org

Collective establishes digital headquarters at named domain

NEW ORLEANS, LA — June 10, 2026 — The Ruff Cutz Ethical Musical Collective ("Ruff Cutz," "the Collective") today confirmed the establishment of an official web presence located at ruffcutz.org. The website is accessible via the address ruffcutz.org.

"The website is now live at ruffcutz.org," said a spokesperson for the Collective. "Stakeholders may reach it by entering ruffcutz.org into a web browser of their choosing."

The domain, ruffcutz.org, was selected following an internal evaluation process. The Collective declined to comment on the alternative domains considered.

The website offers visitors the ability to learn about the Collective, subscribe to SMS updates, and, as of today, read official press releases. Additional functionality has not been ruled out.

The Collective confirmed that ruffcutz.org is expected to remain the address of the website for the foreseeable future.

About Ruff Cutz Ethical Musical Collective

Ruff Cutz is a New Orleans-based musical outfit playing tunes that make you feel. The Collective remains committed to its mission of music for the movement. Recordings are available on Bandcamp at turbozone.bandcamp.com.
