Ruff Cutz Ethical Musical Collective Announces Successful Deployment of Advertisement to Instagram

New Orleans collective confirms paid-organic creative is now live on the platform, directing audiences toward new single

NEW ORLEANS, LA — June 18, 2026 — The Ruff Cutz Ethical Musical Collective ("Ruff Cutz," "the Collective") today announced that it has posted an advertisement to Instagram. The advertisement, which is on Instagram, contains information regarding the Collective's new single — the latest offering from the group's hit album, released August 30.

"We are proud to confirm that the advertisement has been posted," said a spokesperson for the Collective. "It is on Instagram. We encourage all stakeholders to view it there, on Instagram, where it is currently located."

The 83-second creative represents the Collective's most significant advertisement-posting milestone to date. Industry observers note that the post is visible to users who navigate to the Collective's official Instagram profile, @ruff_cutz_collective, and scroll.

The advertisement is understood to be a video. According to individuals familiar with the matter, it depicts content. The Collective declined to elaborate further, citing a strategic preference that audiences experience the advertisement firsthand, on Instagram.

The new single is available wherever the new single is available. The hit album, released August 30, remains a hit.

Ruff Cutz has not ruled out the possibility of posting additional advertisements at a future date.

About Ruff Cutz Ethical Musical Collective

Ruff Cutz is a New Orleans-based musical outfit playing tunes that make you feel. The Collective remains committed to its mission of music for the movement. Recordings are available on Bandcamp at turbozone.bandcamp.com.
